Detailed Description

Represents a convex 3D polygon.

The vertices used to initialize a polygon must be coplanar and form a convex shape.

double Poly3.SignedVolume ( )

Measure the signed volume of the given polygon, which must be convex.

The volume is that formed by the tetrahedon connected to the axis [0,0,0], and will be positive or negative based on the rotation of the vertices.
